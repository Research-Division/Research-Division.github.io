Global Trade Patterns

These visualizations show global trade patterns across different commodity sections and countries in 2024. The relative size of the country's node represents the share of US imports or US exports that country represents. Data within the treemap is given such that at the country-continent level the percent is the share of total Imports or Exports that country comprises, and the value within the parentheses is the dollar value of imports/exports represented by that country.

By clicking on any given country, you can 'drill-down' to the country-product level. Each node at this level represents the share of a given country's imports/exports to the US are comprised of that particular commodity. Commodities are given at the HS-2 chapter level, and colors are dictated by the broader HS section. You can navigate back to the global level using the reset chart icon () at the top of each chart.
